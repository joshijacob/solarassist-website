What is Solar AMC?

A Solar Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC) ensures your solar power plant operates at peak efficiency throughout the year with scheduled inspections, preventive maintenance, and priority technical support.

Scheduled Maintenance

Periodic system inspection and preventive maintenance visits.

Priority Breakdown Support

Faster response time for inverter or generation issues.

Performance Monitoring

Identification of generation loss and corrective action.

What Our AMC Covers

Solar Panels

  • Visual inspection
  • Mounting & structure check
  • Basic cleaning guidance

Inverters & Electricals

  • Error diagnostics
  • DC/AC wiring inspection
  • Protection checks

Monitoring Support

  • App & portal issues
  • Generation analysis
  • Alert troubleshooting
