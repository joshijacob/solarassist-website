About Solar Assist

Our Story

Solar Assist is a pure solar service provider based in Changanacherry, Kottayam, dedicated to maintaining and optimizing existing solar power systems.

We focus exclusively on solar maintenance services, bringing specialized expertise to ensure your solar investment continues to deliver maximum returns.

Unlike companies that focus on new installations, we specialize exclusively in maintaining, optimizing, and repairing existing solar systems to ensure they operate at peak efficiency throughout their lifespan.

Why Maintenance Matters

  • Regular maintenance can increase system efficiency by 15-25%
  • Proper care extends system lifespan by 5-10 years
  • Prevents costly breakdowns and replacements
  • Ensures maximum return on your solar investment

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide exceptional solar maintenance services that extend the lifespan and efficiency of solar power systems across Kerala, helping customers maximize their renewable energy investment.
