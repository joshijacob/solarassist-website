About Solar Assist

Solar Assist is a dedicated solar maintenance and support company serving Kottayam and Pathanamthitta districts in Kerala.

Who We Are

Solar Assist was founded with a simple mission — to help solar system owners achieve reliable performance, long life, and optimal generation from their solar installations. We understand that even a small issue like a dirty panel or inverter error can lead to significant loss in efficiency and savings.

Our team consists of trained technicians with hands-on field experience in solar inspection, maintenance, troubleshooting and monitoring support across residential and commercial systems.
