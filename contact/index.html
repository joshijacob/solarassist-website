Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide solar service for systems installed by other companies?

Yes. Solar Assist provides maintenance, cleaning, inverter repair and monitoring support for systems installed by any company.

Which districts do you currently serve?

We currently provide services in Kottayam and Pathanamthitta districts. Expansion to nearby districts is planned.

Do you support all inverter brands?

We support most major inverter brands including Growatt, SMA, Fronius, Sungrow, Solis, Sofar, Enphase, Delta and Deye.

How quickly can you attend a service request?

For most cases, we respond within 24–48 hours. AMC customers receive priority support.

Do you offer Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC)?

Yes. We offer flexible AMC plans covering inspections, troubleshooting, and performance support.

How can I book a service?

You can call us directly, WhatsApp us, or submit the contact form on this page.
